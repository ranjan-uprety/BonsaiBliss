The Benefits of Bonsai to Reduce Stress & Anxiety

November 16, 2022 by Usha Balasubramanyan

Src:www.homelane.com

Bonsai is a Japanese art form that has been used for centuries to reduce stress and anxiety. It involves carefully pruning and styling trees and shrubs in a container,... which can create a calming, peaceful atmosphere. Bonsai has become increasingly popular in recent years as people are looking for ways to reduce stress and anxiety without the use of medication. Bonsai provides an outlet for creativity, reduces stress levels, improves concentration, and helps with relaxation. It also has the potential to help with physical health issues such as high blood pressure, chronic pain, and insomnia. By practicing bonsai regularly, you can experience these benefits while improving your mental health.

The Negative Effects of Bonsai on Natural Resources

December 22, 2022 by Vastu

Src:www.livspace.com

Bonsai trees are becoming increasingly popular as a decorative element in homes and gardens. However, this trend has also had some negative impacts on... our natural resources. The bonsai industry has led to the over-harvesting of wild trees, deforestation and soil erosion. In addition to this, bonsai requires a lot of water and fertilizer which can lead to water pollution if not managed properly. As such, it is important that we consider the environmental impacts of bonsai before we decide to grow them in our homes or gardens.
